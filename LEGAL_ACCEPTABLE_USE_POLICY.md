# CiteStack Acceptable Use Policy (Draft)

**Last updated:** 2026-02-10

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") describes prohibited conduct when using CiteStack.

## You may not use CiteStack to
- Break the law or encourage illegal activity.
- Upload, store, or distribute malware or harmful code.
- Attempt unauthorized access to accounts, data, systems, or networks.
- Interfere with or disrupt the Service (e.g., excessive automated requests, denial-of-service).
- Scrape or collect personal data about others without a lawful basis.
- Upload content that infringes intellectual property rights when you do not have permission.
- Use the Service to generate, store, or distribute content that is harassing, threatening, or hateful.

## Automated usage
- You may not run bots or automation that overloads the Service.
- We may apply rate limits, job limits, or other controls.

## Enforcement
We may investigate suspected violations and suspend or terminate accounts where appropriate.
